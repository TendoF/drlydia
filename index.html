About Dr Mazhude

Dr Lydia Mazhude is a General Practitioner based at Belvedere Medical Centre, 189 Samora Machel Avenue, Belvedere, Harare. She holds a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBChB) from the University of Zimbabwe.

She provides comprehensive primary medical care for adults and children — evaluating acute illness, managing chronic conditions, performing routine check-ups, and referring patients to specialists when needed.

Core Duties & Services

Dr Mazhude provides a wide range of general medical services at Belvedere Medical Centre. Click any category to learn more.

Diagnoses Illnesses

Evaluating acute symptoms like infections, body aches, respiratory issues, and minor injuries.

Manages Chronic Conditions

Monitoring and managing hypertension, diabetes, asthma, and other long-term conditions.

Prescribes Medication

Writing prescriptions, monitoring treatment plans, and adjusting dosages as needed.

Routine Check-ups

Wellness assessments, physical examinations, and routine health screenings.

Specialist Referrals

Assessing complex cases and referring patients to specialists or advanced testing facilities.

Family Medicine

General care for patients of all ages, from children to adults and the elderly.

Our Approach to Care

Every consultation begins with a thorough clinical history and physical examination to identify the root cause of your concern — not just the symptoms. Dr Mazhude builds individualised care plans combining accurate diagnosis, appropriate treatment, and ongoing monitoring.

Patients are treated with dignity, patience, and clear communication about their diagnosis and treatment options.
